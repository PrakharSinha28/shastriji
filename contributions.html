Shastri ji was the 2nd Prime Minister of Independent India and here are some of his contributions:


  1. Lal Bahadur Shastri was a strong supporter of White Revolution, a national campaignto increase milk production and distribution
  2. In 1965, Shastriji played a key role in promoting the green revolution, whic helped India ncrease agricultural production and moved towards self-sufficiency in food grains.
  3. During shastriji's rule, India went to war with Pakistan over kashmir in 1965 and reached upto lahore in Pakistan. He gave the slogan of 'JAI JAWAN ,JAI KISAAN', valueing the soldiers and farmers of the country.
  4. During 1965 Indo-Pakistan war, India faced a shortage of food and was forced to import food from other countries. At this difficult time Shastriji gave call for self reliance and asked the public including his family to skip a meal once a week as a part of national wide effort, public reacted enthusiastically and many restaurants were closed on monday evenings.
